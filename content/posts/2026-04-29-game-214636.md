A playable demo of Final Fantasy 7 Rebirth is now available for download on Nintendo Switch 2 and Xbox Series X/S, providing gamers with a taste of what to expect from the highly anticipated sequel. Set to release on June 3, this demo allows players to experience Chapters 1 and 2, featuring several hours of gameplay that includes combat, exploration, and side content.

The demo showcases key moments such as the iconic Nibelheim flashback with Cloud and Sephiroth, as well as the expansive Grasslands open region. Players can also experiment with the new Synergy Abilities in the battle system and utilize the Streamlined Progression features, which simplify gameplay by offering unlimited HP and MP, an always-full ATB gauge, and easier weapon ability acquisition.

Notably, players who engage with the demo can carry their save data to the full game and unlock exclusive bonuses, including a Kupo Charm and a Survival Set. With pre-orders now available at a 20% discount until June 10, fans are eager to dive into this latest chapter of the Final Fantasy saga.
