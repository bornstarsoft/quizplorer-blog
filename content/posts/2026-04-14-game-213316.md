Nintendo has delighted Animal Crossing fans by sending a nostalgic surprise to players of New Horizons in celebration of the series' 25th anniversary. This special occasion not only marks a significant milestone for the beloved franchise but also showcases Nintendo's commitment to engaging its community through thoughtful updates and gifts.

To commemorate this anniversary, players received a Leaf Statue along with a heartfelt letter inspired by the original Nintendo 64 version of Dbutsu no Mori. This gesture highlights the deep-rooted connection players have with the series, reminding them of the joy and creativity that Animal Crossing has brought into their lives over the years.

Additionally, the release of update 3.0.2 brings bug fixes and new music from the original GameCube soundtrack, further enhancing the nostalgic experience.
