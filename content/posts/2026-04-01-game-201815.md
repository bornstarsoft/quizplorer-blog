NIS America has just announced an exciting addition to the gaming world with the release of R-Type Tactics I  II Cosmos, set to launch on June 18, 2026. This collection brings the beloved turn-based strategy gameplay of the classic R-Type Tactics titles to modern consoles, including Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC. With the chance to control both the Space Corps and the Bydo Empire, players can expect a unique and immersive experience that expands the R-Type universe.

The game features multiple campaigns and branching storylines, allowing players to explore the depths of the Bydo Empire while engaging in tactical battles. Not only does this release mark the debut of R-Type Tactics II in the West, but it also introduces a brand-new post-game story, ensuring that fans have plenty of content to dive into. With hundreds of ships and levels to choose from, R-Type Tactics I  II Cosmos promises to deliver an unparalleled strategic experience.

As we look forward to its release, one can't help but wonder how this revival of a classic will resonate with both long-time fans and newcomers alike. Will R-Type Tactics I  II Cosmos redefine turn-based strategy for a new generation? Only time will tell.
