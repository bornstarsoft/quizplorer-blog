If you enjoy puzzle games but prefer a more relaxed experience, then Botany Manor might just be your next favorite title. Currently available for free on the Epic Games Store, this cozy first-person puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene, exploring a Victorian manor filled with botanical mysteries. Unlike many challenging puzzle games that can leave players feeling frustrated, Botany Manor strikes a balance between engaging puzzles and a soothing atmosphere, making it accessible for casual gamers.

The games lead developer, Laure De May, emphasized the importance of creating a peaceful setting while ensuring the puzzles are neither too easy nor overly difficult. This thoughtful design allows players to enjoy the journey of discovery without the pressure of high-stakes challenges. With positive reviews highlighting its appeal to casual gamers, Botany Manor is an excellent choice for those who want to unwind while engaging their minds.

As this is the first time Botany Manor is offered for free, its a perfect opportunity to dive into a charming world of puzzles without any cost. Will you take a break from the more intense puzzle games and give Botany Manor a try?
