Apple Arcade has just expanded its library with two remarkable indie titles: Dredge and Unpacking. Both games have been meticulously optimized for mobile devices, ensuring that players can enjoy these unique experiences on the go. Dredge+ offers a chilling blend of fishing and horror, while Unpacking+ transforms the simple act of arranging items into a heartfelt narrative.

Dredge+ is an intriguing mix of gameplay mechanics, featuring fishing minigames during the day and spine-tingling encounters with Lovecraftian monsters at night. This duality not only keeps players engaged but also evokes a sense of psychological tension reminiscent of classic horror games. On the other hand, Unpacking+ takes a minimalist approach, allowing players to unpack and arrange their belongings in a new home, all while unraveling a touching story. Its success at the 2023 App Store Awards highlights its cultural impact and appeal.
