In a thrilling announcement, SEGA is gearing up to celebrate the 35th anniversary of Puyo Puyo in 2026, alongside Sonic the Hedgehog's milestone. The festivities will kick off with the Dairensa! project, a nod to a beloved song from the puzzle game series, promising fans a plethora of exciting initiatives and events.

On February 1, 2026, SEGA will host a special live stream featuring voice actors, live demos, and updates on the mobile game Puyo Puyo!! Quest. Additionally, fans can look forward to pop-up stores in Japan and collaborative events that will bring the community together in celebration. With limited-edition merchandise and a lottery featuring exclusive prizes, this anniversary is set to be a memorable occasion for both new and longtime fans.

As SEGA continues to unveil plans for this dual celebration, it raises the question: what other surprises might they have in store for fans of these iconic franchises? Stay tuned for more updates as we approach these exciting anniversaries!
