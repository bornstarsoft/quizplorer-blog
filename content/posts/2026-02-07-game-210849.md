In a significant move for the gaming industry, JetSynthesys has acquired EverMerge, a top-grossing merge puzzle game that has generated over $350 million in lifetime revenue. This acquisition marks a pivotal step in JetSynthesys' strategy to expand its global gaming ecosystem, particularly targeting high-growth regions like India. With EverMerge's proven success in Western markets, the company aims to leverage its expertise in community engagement and user acquisition to introduce the game to new audiences.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, emphasized the importance of this acquisition in bridging global intellectual properties with local insights. The goal is to resonate with players through unique cultural storytelling and innovative technology. As JetSynthesys continues to invest in EverMerge, the focus will be on expanding its content and delivering fresh experiences that keep gameplay engaging and fun.

This acquisition not only highlights JetSynthesys' commitment to becoming a leading global games company but also reflects the growing potential of the gaming market in emerging economies. How will this strategic move influence the landscape of mobile gaming in India and beyond?
