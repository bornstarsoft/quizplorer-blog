Bloober Team, the renowned Polish game developer, is gearing up for a significant year ahead, promising to unveil several new projects that fans have eagerly anticipated. CEO Piotr Babieno has indicated that 2026 will be a year of intense communication, with announcements expected at various industry events. This marks a pivotal moment for the studio, known for its immersive horror experiences, as they prepare to share insights into their project pipeline.

Among the exciting developments is Project H, an ambitious new title currently in pre-production by the team that brought us Cronos: The New Dawn. Babieno has described Project H as the most ambitious project the team has ever undertaken, with the highest commercial potential of any of their titles. This signals a bold step forward for Bloober Team as they expand their portfolio and continue to captivate audiences with innovative gameplay.
