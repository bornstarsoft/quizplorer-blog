If youve ever dreamed of seeing your beloved pet transformed into a whimsical character, nows your chance! Merge Donuts, the charming Suika-style puzzle game, is hosting its first-ever Furry Friends fan contest, inviting players to submit photos or videos of their pets for a shot at donut immortality. This unique opportunity not only celebrates our furry companions but also integrates them into the delightful chaos of the game.

Running from February 18 to March 4, the contest encourages pet owners to showcase their pets in creative ways. By posting on Instagram or TikTok with the required hashtags and tagging the games creators, participants can enter for a chance to have their pets reimagined as collectible donut characters within the game. With up to 12 winners being selected, this contest is a sweet way to engage with the Merge Donuts community while giving your pet a moment in the spotlight.

As we embrace this fun and interactive contest, its a reminder of how gaming can bring us closer to our pets and foster creativity. Will you be entering your furry friend into the contest?
