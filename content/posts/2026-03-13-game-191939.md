The upcoming pixel art block-pushing puzzle game, Hazard Pay, is set to launch this June for PlayStation 5, Switch, and PC, and it promises to deliver a unique blend of stealth, survival horror, and narrative choice. Set in a chilling Cold War-era research facility, players will navigate through handcrafted puzzle levels while uncovering dark secrets behind a catastrophic containment breach. The game invites players to engage in a tense clean-up operation, where every decision can alter the fate of trapped scientists and the outcome of the story.

With its ingenious puzzle mechanics and environmental storytelling, Hazard Pay stands out as a compelling addition to the indie gaming landscape. The games ability to blend engaging gameplay with a rich narrative is sure to resonate with fans of both puzzle and horror genres. As players prepare to dive into this eerie world, one must wonder: will you become just another disposable tool of the system, or will you be the one to expose the hidden horrors within?
