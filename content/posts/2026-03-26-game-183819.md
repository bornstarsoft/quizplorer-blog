Agni-Flare Co., Ltd. has officially opened pre-orders for its highly anticipated ultra-high-speed competitive puzzle game, SHIKA-Q, on the Nintendo Switch. This exciting announcement comes as the game gears up for a worldwide release on April 9, 2026, at 00:00 local time. With its unique blend of fast-paced gameplay and competitive elements, SHIKA-Q is set to capture the attention of puzzle enthusiasts and gamers alike.

In addition to the Nintendo Switch, SHIKA-Q will also be available on PlayStation4, PlayStation5, and Steam, with the latter's release date yet to be announced. The game promises to deliver an exhilarating experience that challenges players' reflexes and strategic thinking. As we await its launch, fans can look forward to more updates and teasers from Agni-Flare, ensuring that the excitement continues to build.
