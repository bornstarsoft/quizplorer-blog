In the world of indie gaming, few titles manage to blend emotional storytelling with engaging gameplay as effectively as "Save My Scrap." Set to launch on April 22, 2026, on Steam, this visual novel and puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of a mechanic tasked with repairing a broken android named Harima. What starts as a simple mechanical job evolves into a profound exploration of memories, connections, and the essence of humanity.

Developed by Unio Anou and published by AMATA Games, "Save My Scrap" is an upgraded version of the popular free game "Fix My Junk." This definitive edition promises new dialogues, endings, and a gallery mode, enhancing the player experience while retaining the heart-wrenching narrative that captivated fans in the first place. With multilingual support and a comfortable gameplay experience, it aims to reach a global audience eager for rich storytelling.

As we approach the release date, the anticipation builds. Will players be able to navigate the emotional complexities of repairing not just an android, but also the relationships intertwined with it? "Save My Scrap" is more than just a game; it's a journey that challenges our perceptions of connection and memory. Are you ready to dive into this heartfelt adventure?
