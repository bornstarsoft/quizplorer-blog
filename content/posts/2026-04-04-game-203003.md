On April 8, 2026, gamers will be treated to the launch of "Good Luck," a physics-based urban survival game that promises to deliver chaos and hilarity. Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games, this game stands out for its unique blend of urban survival mechanics and the unpredictable nature of rage games. With the ability to play solo or in an online co-op mode with up to five players, the game is set to redefine multiplayer experiences in a lighthearted yet challenging environment.

"Good Luck" invites players to navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards, where even the most mundane objects can become deadly obstacles. The absence of checkpoints adds an extra layer of challenge, pushing players to master the chaotic physics and work together to reach their ultimate goal: a meeting that seems almost impossible to attend. As the game launches on PC via Steam, followed by releases on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, it raises the question: will you survive the urban jungle?
