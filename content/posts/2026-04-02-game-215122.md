Minos, launching on April 9, reimagines the classic Greek myth of the Minotaur, offering a fresh take on strategy puzzle gameplay. Players are tasked with controlling the labyrinth itself, protecting Asterion, the Minotaur, from invading heroes. This innovative twist allows players to shape the maze, using walls, gates, and corridors to thwart intruders seeking glory.

The game emphasizes labyrinth-building mechanics, where players can design intricate paths, split enemy groups, and utilize shifting layouts to confuse their foes. With Daedalus as the maze's creator, players will uncover the story while feeding the sentient labyrinth with the blood of fallen adventurers. Each run introduces new enemies and traps, ensuring that no two nights are the same and keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging.

As we anticipate the release of Minos, its exciting to see how this unique blend of strategy and mythology will resonate with gamers. Will you take on the challenge of defending the Minotaur's sanctuary?
