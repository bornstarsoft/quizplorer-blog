If you're a PlayStation Plus subscriber, there's a hidden gem you absolutely need to check out: The Exit 8. This free puzzle game, recently added to the PS Plus Extra and Premium tiers, has been garnering attention for its unique blend of horror and puzzle-solving that keeps players on their toes. With its eerie atmosphere and intriguing gameplay mechanics, it's no wonder that players are raving about this indie title.

Developed by Kotake Create, The Exit 8 immerses you in a first-person experience set in a looping Japanese metro tunnel. Your mission? Identify various anomalies that range from harmless discrepancies to spine-chilling visuals. The game's premise is simple yet effective: spot the anomalies and turn around to progress. It may only take a couple of hours to complete, but the thrill of uncovering each anomaly makes it a memorable experience.

As players dive into this short yet creepy adventure, many express a desire for more content, highlighting the game's engaging mix of obvious and subtle challenges. If you're looking for a unique gaming experience that combines horror elements with puzzle-solving, The Exit 8 is definitely worth your time. Will you dare to uncover the mysteries lurking in the shadows?
