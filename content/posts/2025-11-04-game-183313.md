As we approach the end of 2025, the Nintendo Switch 2 is making waves in the gaming community, particularly with its nominations at the prestigious Golden Joystick Awards. Notably, two of its flagship titles, Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza, have been shortlisted for the coveted 'Console Game of the Year' award. This recognition not only highlights the quality of these games but also sets a promising tone for the future of the Switch 2 and its growing library of titles.

The competition is fierce, with heavyweights like Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Ytei also in the running. However, the fact that both Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza are being recognized in multiple categories, including 'Best Multiplayer' and 'Best Audio Design', speaks volumes about their impact on the gaming landscape. As fans eagerly await the announcement of winners on November 20th, its clear that the Switch 2 is already carving out its place in gaming history.

With voting open to the public until the end of the month, gamers have the opportunity to support their favorites. Will the Switch 2 continue to dominate the awards scene, or will other titles steal the spotlight? Only time will tell!
