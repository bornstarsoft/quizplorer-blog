Players will navigate through four interconnected worlds, each filled with unique mechanics and narratives, using a diverse cast of characters that include a queen, a thief, and even a talking boat.

What sets Order of the Sinking Star apart is its collaborative spirit, drawing from the expertise of various game developers who have contributed to the Sokoban genre. This fusion of ideas is expected to create a multilayered experience that challenges players in new and exciting ways. As we await its release in 2026, the anticipation builds for how this game will push the boundaries of puzzle-solving and storytelling in gaming.
