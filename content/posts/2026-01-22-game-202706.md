Nintendo has just rolled out a significant free update for Donkey Kong Country Returns HD, enhancing the experience on both the Switch and the newly launched Switch 2. The highlight of this update is the addition of Dixie Kong as a playable character, bringing fresh gameplay dynamics that allow players to explore the game in exciting new ways. With her unique ability to spin her ponytail while jumping, Dixie can reach higher platforms and traverse levels more effectively than Diddy Kong.

In addition to the new character, the update introduces a Turbo Attack mode, allowing players to race through stages at an accelerated pace. This mode not only adds a thrilling challenge but also rewards players with turbo medals for completing courses within the time limit. Switch users will benefit from exclusive enhancements, including improved render resolution and shorter loading times, making the gameplay experience smoother than ever.
