The upcoming indie game, Hakoniwa Electric, promises to be a delightful escape into a cozy isometric world filled with charming puzzles. Developed by Anettai, this game invites players to unwind as they help Akari, the new shopkeeper, manage her grandfather's electronics store while solving whimsical wiring puzzles. Set for release in 2026, it offers a unique blend of relaxation and exploration that is sure to resonate with fans of the genre.

Players will navigate through 12 beautifully crafted stages, each reflecting the changing seasons and filled with intricate details. The puzzles are designed to be accessible, allowing players to engage without the stress of time limits or complex calculations. Instead, the focus is on enjoying the process of discovery and the satisfaction of illuminating the miniature world around them.

As Akari chronicles her experiences in her Monthly Diary, players can look forward to a gentle narrative that unfolds over the course of a year. This game not only invites players to solve puzzles but also to immerse themselves in a heartwarming story. With its charming aesthetics and relaxing gameplay, Hakoniwa Electric is poised to become a beloved title in the indie game community. Will you be ready to light up the world when it launches?
