FuRyu, the renowned developer behind the acclaimed Caligula Effect series, has set the gaming community abuzz with a mysterious teaser for their upcoming console game, Project Alice. The official X account launched on April 22 features an eerie audio clip of a girls voice repeating Im sorry in a haunting manner, hinting at a narrative steeped in angst and possibly horror. This cryptic approach not only intrigues fans but also invites speculation about the game's themes and mechanics.

Scheduled for a follow-up announcement on April 25, 2026, at 20:30 JST, FuRyu is encouraging fans to engage with the teaser by piecing together clues that will be revealed in the days leading up to the big reveal. The dark visuals accompanying the audio, including a crying eye, further amplify the suspense surrounding this new project.
