Sony's recent State of Play presentation focused exclusively on games from Japan and Asia, delivering a whopping 28 announcements in just 40 minutes. Among the highlights were exciting reveals like the remake of Dragon Quest VII, set to launch on February 5, 2026, and the return of the iconic Tokyo Xtreme Racer series, marking its comeback after two decades.

The showcase also featured a mix of indie gems and major titles, including new DLC for Gran Turismo 7 and the highly anticipated Elden Ring Nightreign expansion. With such a diverse lineup, it's clear that Sony is committed to bringing a rich variety of gaming experiences to its audience.
