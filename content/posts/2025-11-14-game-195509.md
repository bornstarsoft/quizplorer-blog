Netflix is diving into the daily puzzle game trend with its upcoming app, Netflix Puzzled. This innovative platform will feature puzzles inspired by some of Netflix's most beloved franchises, including Stranger Things, Bridgerton, and Squid Game. Subscribers can look forward to a variety of classic and fresh puzzle formats, such as sudoku, crosswords, and word searches, all designed to engage fans in a new way.

The app is set to launch soon on both iOS and Android, and it will also be the first Netflix game available on the Tudum website. Best of all, Netflix Puzzled will be free for all subscribers, making it an exciting addition to the streaming giant's expanding gaming portfolio. With the previous success of interactive content, this move could redefine how fans interact with their favorite shows.
