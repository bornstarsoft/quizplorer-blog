The newly launched visual puzzle game AiliA, developed by IceToad Studio LLC, invites players to dive into a captivating world where mirrors are not just reflections but gateways to new challenges. Priced at $11.99 with a limited-time 20% discount, AiliA offers a unique gameplay experience that combines 3D puzzle-solving with innovative mirror mechanics.

Players will step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blur the lines between reality and reflection. With over 100 puzzles to solve, including intricate water reflections and recursive structures, AiliA promises to engage both casual gamers and puzzle enthusiasts alike. The game also features a special collaboration bundle with Moncage, providing additional value for fans of the genre.

As you explore the enchanting worlds of AiliA, you'll uncover the intertwined stories of sisters Aria and Lia, making each puzzle not just a challenge, but a piece of a larger narrative. Will you be able to manipulate the mirrors and reveal the secrets hidden within?
