PEARL ABYSS has made headlines with its open-world action-adventure game Crimson Desert, which has impressively surpassed 5 million copies sold globally in just 26 days. This remarkable achievement not only highlights the game's popularity but also sets a new record for the fastest sales pace in Korean console game history. The developers expressed their gratitude to the community, acknowledging the support from players who have explored the vast world of Pywel and created their unique narratives.

To put this milestone into perspective, last year's Game of the Year winner, Clair Obscur: Expedition 33, took five months to reach the same sales figure. This rapid success for Crimson Desert indicates a significant shift in the gaming landscape, where players are increasingly drawn to immersive worlds and engaging gameplay. As the gaming industry continues to evolve, it will be fascinating to see how other titles respond to this new benchmark. Will we see more games breaking sales records in the near future?
