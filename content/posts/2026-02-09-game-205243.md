Get ready to dive into a whimsical world of creativity and problem-solving with the upcoming release of "HER TREES: PUZZLE DREAM" on February 10, 2026. This point-and-click puzzle game, developed by the solo creator stone, invites players to engage with an innovative puzzle system where the solutions emerge through the thoughtful arrangement of various objects, such as scraps of paper and leaves. Unlike traditional puzzle games, players can freely drag and drop items, allowing for a unique and relaxing gameplay experience that encourages exploration and inspiration.

As the third installment in the HER TREES series, this game builds on the success of its predecessor, "HER TREES: THE PUZZLE HOUSE," which garnered a "Very Positive" rating on Steam. The games non-verbal design and black-and-white aesthetic ensure accessibility for all players, regardless of color vision.
